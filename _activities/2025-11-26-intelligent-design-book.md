参与编写了教育部碳中和城市与绿色智慧建筑系列教材之一的《智慧设计与环境交互》，负责第三章部分内容的撰写与整理工作。  

参与研发的相关计算性设计工具原型[SIMForms](../projects/2023-10-12-simforms)、[FLEXUrban](../projects/2024-11-13-flexurban)等，作为教材配套的数字资源一并发布，用于支持相关教学与实践应用。

Contributed to the textbook ***Intelligent Design and Interactive Environment***, part of the Ministry of Education’s “Carbon-Neutral Cities and Green Intelligent Buildings” textbook series. I participated in the writing and compilation of selected sections of **Chapter 3**.

In addition, computational design tool prototypes developed during my research, including [SIMForms](../projects/2023-10-12-simforms) and [FLEXUrban](../projects/2024-11-13-flexurban), were released as accompanying digital resources to support teaching and practical applications related to the textbook.

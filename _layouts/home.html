Welcome to my blog!

My name is Baizhou Zhang (张柏洲), a PhD candidate at School of Architecture, Southeast University. My research focuses on computational design, generative design, and AI applications in architecture.

Main contributor of SIMForms, FLEXUrban, and other generative tools for architectural and urban design, dedicated to synchronizing computational technologies with designers' thinking.

Honored with the Young CAADRIA Award and served as a reviewer for CAADRIA. Received funding from the Postgraduate Research & Practice Innovation Program of Jiangsu Province and SEU Innovation Capability Enhancement Plan for Doctoral Students.

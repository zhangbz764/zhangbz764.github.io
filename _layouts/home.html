Welcome to my academic blog!

My name is Baizhou Zhang (张柏洲), and I am currently a PhD candidate at Inst. AAA, Southeast University. My research focuses on generative design, parametric design and application of AI in architecture.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Architecture from School of Architecture, Southeast University. Then I was enrolled in a direct PhD program at Southeast University, where I completed the coursework and research requirements for a Master's degree but transitioned directly into the PhD phase to further my studies.

I was honored with the Young CAADRIA Award and received funding twice from the Postgraduate Research & Practice Innovation Program of Jiangsu Province. These will further motivate me to continue my research in this field.
